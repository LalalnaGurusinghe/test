locations

make your destination

colombo

Welcome to Colombo! Explore the vibrant capital of Sri Lanka, where history meets modernity. Discover its charm through bustling markets, serene temples, and pristine beaches. Start your adventure now!

kandy

Welcome to Kandy, the cultural heart of Sri Lanka!Sri Lanka's cultural gem! Explore sacred temples, lush landscapes, and vibrant traditions in this historic city nestled among green hills.

anuradhapura

Welcome to Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Sri Lanka, offers a glimpse into a civilization over two millennia old. Explore ancient ruins and serene lakes, immersing yourself in its spiritual and historical richness.

polonnaruwa

Welcome to Polonnaruwa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Sri Lanka, blends ancient history with natural beauty. Explore ruins of palaces, temples, and statues from the Polonnaruwa Kingdom. Amid lush greenery and tranquil reservoirs, experience the timeless charm of this cultural treasure.

galle

Welcome to Galle, a coastal city in Sri Lanka, is famed for its 17th-century Dutch fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore its historic streets, lined with charming boutiques and cafes. Beyond the fort, pristine beaches offer relaxation and water sports. Galle is a vibrant blend of history and coastal beauty.

mirissa

Welcome to Mirissa, a coastal gem in Sri Lanka, boasts pristine beaches and a relaxed atmosphere. Enjoy surfing, snorkeling, and whale watching by day, and savor fresh seafood with ocean views by night. It's the perfect destination for tropical relaxation and beachside bliss.

ella

welcome to Ella, a picturesque town in Sri Lanka's hill country, offers stunning views of tea plantations, waterfalls, and misty mountains. Hike to Ella Rock or visit the Nine Arch Bridge for iconic vistas. Relax with a cup of Ceylon tea amidst serene surroundings. It's a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventurers.

horton plains

welcome to Horton Plains, Sri Lanka's captivating natural reserve, offers diverse landscapes perfect for hiking and nature exploration. Visit World's End for panoramic views and Baker's Falls for cascading beauty. It's an unforgettable adventure in untouched wilderness.
